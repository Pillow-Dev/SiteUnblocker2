The most comprehensive list of the best hosting WordPress themes designed and developed for shared web hosting, managed hosting, VPS and dedicated server providers, as well as domain registrars.

If you are looking for a fun way to sort out your hosting website, you better not miss choosing a tool from the list below. Every option offers you a plethora of features and functions that help you kick-start your web hosting project in no time at all.

In short, let the machines do the heavy lifting for you. For you, well, you just focus on the finishing touches and you are ready to roll.

Make the best hosting website with a WordPress theme

1. Choose the design wisely

Frankly, you do not really need to invest too much thought into this one. After all, when using any of the themes from the list here, they all promise a well-thought-look that will wow all your visitors and potential customers/clients. In many cases, you will even want to stick to the default appearance, as it already fits your style perfectly.

2. Customization

If you would like to take things to the next level, modifying the theme to your liking, you can do that, too. But let the idea of performing customizations not intimidate you. Luckily, there is visual editing available to every theme via the simplistic method of dragging and dropping. Either the theme uses a popular builder, like Elementor, WPBakery or King Composer, or they even have their custom-made one.

To sum up, you are free to tweak the WordPress theme to the very last detail.

3. User experience

It is important to offer not just a great but excellent user experience with your web hosting website. Even though there is a very good chance that the majority of your users will be using a desktop device to browse your website, it’s still necessary to make it fully compatible with smartphones and tablets.

They may look around the web for a host on a mobile device, but they will later seal the deal on a desktop.

Moreover, you need to take care of fast loading, too. Hey, if a potential client lands on a website of a host and domain registrar and it takes ages to load – will they trust them? Of course not.

Needless to say, with a predefined theme, you need not worry about any of the above. They are optimized to ensure an outstanding website performance that you and your users deserve.

4. Site navigability

Even though you may be starting slow, at some point, you will surely have many goodies to offer your customers. With that in mind, you need to make sure that all the different services you have available and other whatnot are quick to access, even from a mobile device.

Obviously, you would want to have the main offerings and pricing on the first page to get their hands on the hosting that suits them right from the get-go. But great categorization and menu should also not be omitted. Again, make sure the user experience is always on your mind.

5. WHMCS integration

Many of the hosting WordPress themes also come with the convenient WHMCS integration and WHMCS Bridge. This simplifies the operation of your web hosting and domain registrar business even further. WHMCS stands for “Web Host Manager Complete Solution,” which tells you more than enough.

All of these premium themes come with outstanding quality and pixel perfect design. If you find that some amazing themes are missing, please leave a comment below, and I will list it here.

Of course, if you have some extra questions before purchase, feel free to leave your questions below. I have used several of these themes, and I know what you can and can’t do with them.
