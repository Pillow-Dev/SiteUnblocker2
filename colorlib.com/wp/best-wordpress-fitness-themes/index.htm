A list of best WordPress fitness themes designed and developed especially for gym and fitness center and personal trainer websites.

These are versatile and highly customizable WordPress themes that allow you to make your own unique and professional fitness website you have always wanted.

WordPress is by far the most popular content management system used by 37% of entire websites. It is simple, easy to use and setup and it’s free to use for commercial or any other purpose. Thanks to the incredible community around it, you can get done anything with it without writing your own code.

These fitness themes are a great example of how you can integrate time schedule management, training programs, contact form, trainer pages, and payment systems and customize them via a simple web interface.

Benefits of WordPress fitness themes that do you well

Online schedule

Offer everyone to see what you have going on over at your fitness facility with an online schedule. Showcase what classes are available at what time of the day, and they will have a much easier way of arranging their timing on the fly. If you offer online registrations, this will also come in very handy.

BMI calculator

You can keep your site visitors engaged with all sorts of fun features, of which BMI calculator is one of them. Allow them to fill out the missing information and let the calculator do the rest. Once the result shows, you can add links to different blog posts and other whatnot that could benefit them. Always think of delivering more than you are asking for.

Events

Of course, you want to host different events at your place, right? Not just your location, but other types of events and gatherings, too. With a dedicated section on your website that you will easily update regularly, everyone gets to see what fitness activities are happening around their local area.

Pricing plans

When it comes to fitness and classes, everyone wants to know what’s the cost. Keep everyone familiar with your offerings by introducing pricing plans to your website. For the most part, these WordPress fitness themes sort this out for your out of the box.

Personal trainers

Go on a more personal level with your audience and present who the trainers that work at your fitness center are. Write a compelling bio and even add links to their social profiles. Not only will sports enthusiasts get to see who works at your place, but they will also have a much easier job picking their favorite.

Following modern standards

Even when it comes to a fitness website, it is important to think of the design and performance. This ensures that everyone enjoys your content and has a pleasant experience browsing through your classes, team section and other useful information.

Of course, these WordPress themes make sure that they take care of all the necessary technical and design aspects. In short, very little work is necessary on your end – and you will definitely not need to deal with the tedious coding.

Most likely, there are hundreds or at least dozens of gyms, fitness centers, and CrossFit groups near your location, so you need to stand out.

Creating a unique and professional website might play a crucial role in your fitness business success. Make your website SEO-friendly, and Google will help bring your business in front of the right people.

It all starts with captivating webspace.
