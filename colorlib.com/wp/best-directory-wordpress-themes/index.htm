What to look for in directory WordPress themes

  • Robust and powerful structure that can handle tons of listings, especially when entering a big industry or even starting a generic directory platform. Once people get familiar with your directory, you can quickly have thousands of submissions to review and publish.
  • Mobile compatibility: Browsing products, services, locations, whatever, is highly rewarding if you can do it from your favorite mobile device. Of course, all these high-quality WordPress themes ensure a fluid layout that instantly adapts to any screen size.
  • Easy setup: With a WordPress theme, you can experience a quick and speedy setup that will take you just a few clicks. In short, kick-start your directory project and start working on modifications and additional tweaks right away.
  • Admin/user dashboards: For awesome website management and great user experience, both a powerful admin and a practical user dashboard are a must. With the resourceful alternatives following, you get the best of both, so there’s no need to sort anything out yourself.
  • Search function: Unless it is something super niche, every directory website needs a well-thought-out search function, preferably with advanced options. This allows the user to find what they are searching for much quicker. Along with that, these themes also have great navigability and an option to break things down into different categories.
  • Plans: Whether you are starting a free directory website, a premium one or both, make sure you present pricing plans and have the full members’ area sorted out. Do you need to do it yourself? Of course not; that’s when a theme comes into play.
  • Forms: Another essential feature of a directory/listing WordPress themes are forms, primarily for submissions.
  • Reviews: Focus on credibility with reviews and ratings, as this will help users identify who to trust and work with. Many times, reviews come integrated into the web design, but on all the other occasions, you are welcome to introduce them via a plugin.
  • Make money: There are numerous ways how these WordPress themes allow you to earn money with your directory websites. Advertising, premium bundles and subscriptions, listing submissions, appointments booking and heaps more.
  • Payment gateways: To make the operation of your website run smoothly as butter, different payment gateways come included with the WordPress theme bundle (PayPal, Stripe, etc.).
