  • December 3, 2020

Our comprehensive collection of the best free under construction templates caters to all websites and branding regulations.

Under construction website templates help you to retain customers when you are busy revamping your website. Some of the websites even manage to get many leads before launching their site using the under construction page. For this, you can use a simple opt-in form or newsletter subscription form.

In WordPress, there are plugins to help you create an under construction website page easily. For the people who are using HTML website templates these free under construction website templates will be of great help.

The message you show on the under construction page plays a vital role in engaging the visitors, making them excited for what’s coming next. Tailor the message/information to your audience and make a strong first impression for the new users.

There are other two main elements you must add to the under construction page.

One is a signup form and the other is links to your social media accounts, especially if you are very active on them. But you can also include other details and information, even spice things up with a countdown timer.

Moreover, if only one section of your website is under construction and the rest of the pages are working fine, then provide the alternate page links as well.
