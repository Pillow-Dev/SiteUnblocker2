Top Split-Screen WordPress Themes to Create a Visually Interesting Design

  • February 23, 2021
  • Julia Starlark
  • Theme Collections
  • 0 Comments

Split-screen design is a relatively new trend that is here to stay. Once used widely in the film industry, today split-screen websites are no exception for businesses of all kinds. Think your website could benefit from the split-screen technique? Great! The split-screen WordPress themes below are proof that this web design trend will be popular in the future, too. First, they have a nice aesthetic quality. No matter our niche, your website will offer a wonderful viewing experience. Second, responsive from the ground up, your website can be adapted for a variety of screens. Finally, easy navigation. Clean and easy to navigate your website will encourage users to click.

The split-screen layout can work best for a business that offers two services. As a result, you can add two calls-to-action buttons for more responses. The beauty of premium WordPress themes is simplicity and easiness in usage. So, forget about coding issues and start building new page layouts like a pro. To add, WooCommerce support comes included in the split-screen WordPress themes below. To help your advantage stand out, see features jam-packed in the premium options below. At the end of the day, if the split-screen layout is what your website needs, then what are you waiting for?

The List of Split-Screen WordPress Themes to Explore

Soleng | A Solar Energy Company WordPress Theme

The split-screen layout of Soleng would be a perfect fit for industrial websites. In fact, it’s a multi-skin option that could work for a solar panels solutions agency. It’s built with WPBakery page builder, so it’s easy to skip tricky issues. You just drag and drop things to where you need them and enjoy the result. Thanks to the WooCommerce package, it’s easy to launch an online store and make profits online. Besides, Soleng is compatible with the Events Calendar plugin, which is also a benefit. To begin, explore its creative demos and pick the one that fits your requirements best. To make your pages worth the visit, take advantage of the layouts builder.

Jam-packed with a pack of custom shortcodes & widgets, Insugroup could save you a ton of time. Showcase your skills & achievements in a more vibrant way. Insugroup features several unique demos to help get you started. Want to add more page layouts? A piece of cake. Just get your fingers wet into WPBakery. When it comes to customization, the sky is the limit. Moreover, you can build a multilingual website in the future thanks to the WPML support.
